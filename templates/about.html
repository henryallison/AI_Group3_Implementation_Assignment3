About Malaria and HIV/AIDS AI Chatbot Home

Welcome to the Malaria and HIV/AIDS AI Prediction Chatbot! This chatbot is designed to assist you in the following ways:

  • Predict potential diagnoses of Malaria and HIV/AIDS based on user input symptoms.
  • Provide general medical information about these diseases.
  • Engage in friendly conversations.

Here are some example questions the chatbot can respond to:

  • "What is Malaria?"
  • "What is HIV/AIDS?"
  • "Do HIV/AIDS have a cure?"
  • "Do Malaria have a cure?"
  • "What can I do if I have HIV?"
  • "What can I do if I have Malaria?"
  • "How can I get Malaria?"
  • "How can I prevent Malaria?"
  • "How can I get HIV?"
  • "How can I prevent HIV?"
  • "Hello!" (For friendly greetings)
  • "Bye!" (For farewells)

Feel free to interact with the chatbot and explore its capabilities!
